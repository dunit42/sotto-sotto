Our Story

Fresh. Simple.
Traditional.

Sotto Sotto has been an Atlanta dining destination for over 25 years. Dishes here are firmly rooted in centuries of Italian tradition — from Tortelli di Michelangelo Buonarroti, a 16th-century recipe from the master's own letters, to seasonal risotti prepared with the finest Carnaroli rice.

Chef and owner Riccardo Ullio followed his culinary dreams through the kitchens of Italy's most prestigious restaurants, traveling the country to study its regional cuisines before bringing that knowledge to Atlanta's table.

The majority of our produce, meat, and dairy comes from local farms. Every plate of pasta is handmade in-house. That has never changed.
